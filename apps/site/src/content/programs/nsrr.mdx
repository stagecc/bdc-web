[National Sleep Research Resource (NSRR](https://sleepdata.org/)) is an NHLBI-supported repository for sharing large amounts of sleep data (polysomnography, actigraphy, and questionnaire-based) from multiple cohorts, clinical trials, and other data sources. Launched in April 2014, the mission of the NSRR is to advance sleep and circadian science by supporting secondary data analysis, algorithmic development, and signal processing by sharing high-quality datasets. 

The NSRR encourages active engagement by the sleep and circadian communities, aiming to expand the breadth and depth of the sleep-related data and understand optimal methods of sleep data analysis.
