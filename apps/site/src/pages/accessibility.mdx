BDC is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users. We strive to ensure the BDC website and digital content are usable by the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability.

We aim to conform to the accessibility standards defined by:

* [Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act](https://www.section508.gov/)  
* [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/wcag/)


Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review BDC website content and functionality and work to identify and address accessibility barriers as they are found.

## Report an accessibility issue

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of BDC. If you encounter a barrier or have difficulty using any part of the site, let us know by completing the [Contact Us](/help/contact-and-support) form. If you have difficulty using the form, you may send an email to [biodatacatalyst@nhlbi.nih.gov](mailto:biodatacatalyst@nhlbi.nih.gov). We aim to respond within 2 \- 3 business days.

When reporting an issue, please include:

* The page URL  
* A description of the issue  
* What you were trying to do when the issue occured  
* The assistive technology, device, browser, or operating system you are using, if applicable
