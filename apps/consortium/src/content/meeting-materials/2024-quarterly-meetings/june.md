## Overview

The BioData Catalyst Coordinating Center was proud to host the June '24 BioData Catalyst Face-to-Face meeting in Chapel Hill, NC on June 13 & 14, 2024. 80 people attended the event (41 in person), consisting of BDC consortium members, NHLBI staff, and other partners.
