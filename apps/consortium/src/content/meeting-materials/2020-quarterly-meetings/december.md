## Overview

**ZOOM** was proud to host the ninth BioData Catalyst “Face-to-Face” meeting and the third conducted entirely virtual. 87 BDCatalyst consortium, Fellows, and NHLBI members attended across the two-day meeting focused on Fellows Cohort I feedback, Fellows Cohort II introductions, program partnerships and interoperability, improving the user experience, refining WP3.1 releases, improving project management as well as scaling across all BioData Catalyst initiatives.
